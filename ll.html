About the Project

Warm Hands Project is a youth-led humanitarian initiative committed to providing food, clothing, and emotional support to street children and underprivileged youth across Namibia. Founded by a young changemaker from Northern Namibia who has witnessed these hardships firsthand, the project seeks to restore dignity, hope, and long-term support — not through charity, but through shared accountability and deep community engagement.
